Keeping your information with you

In the modern day, it is incredibly difficult to keep your data private and not be tracked by advertisers. Privacy has virtually become a myth. Peacock hopes to keep your information with you and not let companies steal it and sell it for their personal benefit. Browse the web with confidence knowing that peacock has your back when it comes to analytics, trackers, advertisers, cookies and history.


  • Peacock blocks trackers and analytics
  • Adverts and other popups are blocked too
  • Randomized fingerprints mean anonymized browsing
  • Blocks cookies and deletes history automatically

    • Modern

    Privacy shouldn't mean compromise

    Peacock aims to be a browser that is not only secure but also provides features and options that all mainstream browsers have. Peacock also aims to be a browser that experiments with new features that not many browsers have, providing absolutely no compromise when using it.


  • Can render most sites like the mainstream browsers
  • Has a snappy and modern UI
  • Built on a modified chromium engine
  • Has an active community for support
  • Scores as well as other browsers in benchmarks

    • Tweakable

    Tailor your browser to your taste

    Peacock also aspires to be entirely hackable and customisable by its users, whether you just want to have a browser that works or if you want to change absolutely everything about the UI, peacock will work for you.


  • Entirely tweakable using CSS
  • Theme store with various themes
  • A large amount of settings
  • Provides flags page for extra privacy
  • Open source under the GPLv3 license

    • Beautiful

    Beautiful by default

    Peacock has been redesigned and streamlined to provide a simple modern interface that follows many modern design principles and provides both light and dark themes. A unique new tab page provides you with all the information that you need and helps you to navigate the web.


  • Modern design elements
  • Uses nord theme light and dark
  • Refreshing new tab page to greet you
  • Self-explainatory UI for simple navigation
